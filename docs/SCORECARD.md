## Source confound (the dataset's structural risk)

All normals are YouTube; TikTok/Reddit are 100% fault. Predicting the recording **source** from the embedding alone scores balAcc 0.654±0.08 (MCC 0.561) — the shortcut is *available*. The honest fault/normal number is therefore the **YouTube-only** row above (source held constant), not the all-sources row, whose AUROC is confound-inflated.
